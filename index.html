What's in for StartUps

Onground Visibility

Product showcase & feedback from your Target Audience comprising of college students, corporate professionals, businessmen, investors and entrepreneurs

Investors Trail

Investors from your sector that are present will personally visit your kiosk at different times during the E-Summit to interact with you and know about your firm

Networking Arena

Exclusive Entry to Networking Events - Investors Arena & High Tea. Meet mentors & investors from Matrix Partners, Indian Angels, Sequoia etc as well as speakers.

Offline Publicity

Online Publicity

Presence in social media posts and official website of E-Summit ‘19

Complimentary Passes

5 complimentary Professional Passes which gives you access to all the events to E-Summit ’19.

About StartUp Expo

Startup Expo provides a grand platform for startups to display their product and/ or service to all the people who attend the E-Summit 2018. These people are the potential customers and investors for the startup. Also it provides an opportunity for these startups to network with everyone who attend the Summit. This is also an opportunity for the attendees to learn about the new entrepreneurial techniques that these startups have employed to become successful in their journey.
